Controls

tab start or pause the game

Movement

↑ or w accelerate up

← or a accelerate left

↓ or s accelerate down

→ or d accelerate right

If you want to slow down, just accelerate in the opposite direction. You keep getting faster the longer you accelerate, but it will take just as long to stop.

Shooting

numpad 8 or i shoot up

numpad 4 or j shoot left

numpad 2 or k shoot down

numpad 6 or l shoot right

If your ship is moving, its movement will be added to your bullet's movement when you fire.
